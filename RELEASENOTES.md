# Release Notes

# v2.0.7

## What's New
- **Advanced Render Settings**:
    - **Smart Gradient**: Generate beautiful backgrounds instantly by picking a single color.
    - **Solid Color**: Default set to Black for cleaner starts; fully customizable.
    - **Song Info Customization**: 
        - Choose from multiple positions (Top-Left, Bottom-Center, etc.).
        - Refined styles: 'Classic (Detailed)' and 'Boxed (Floating)'.
        - Adjustable padding/margin to prevent text from hitting screen edges.
    - **Text Animations**: Bring lyrics to life with effects like Bounce, Pulse, Wave, Glitch, Shake, and Typewriter.
    - **Live Font Preview**: Hover over fonts in the list to see them applied instantly.
    - **UI Improvements**: 'Start Render' button now matches the app theme. Relocated 'Font Color' for better workflow.
- **Enhanced Lyric Control**:
    - **Display Modes**: Choose how many lines to show:
        - *Show All (Default)*
        - *Previous - Active - Next*
        - *Next Only*
        - *Active Only*
    - **Custom Preset**: Automatically saves your manual tweaks as a distinct preset.
    - **'None' Preset**: Option to hide all text and overlays completely.
- **Workflow & UI**:
    - **New Shortcut**: Press **'D'** to toggle the Render Settings panel.
    - **Auto-Hide Settings**: Render panel can auto-hide when clicking outside or starting a render.
    - **Dropdowns**: Smoother experience with auto-close on selection for text effects and animations.

## Bug Fixed
- **Lyric Display**: Fixed an issue where "Show All" would sometimes truncate lines during web preview.
- **Render Panel**: Improved visibility behavior ensuring it hides/shows correctly during the render process.
